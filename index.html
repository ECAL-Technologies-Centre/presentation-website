ECALTECH CENTRE

Opening in May 2020

ECALTECH Centre is a place for Deep Learning (AI), augmented reality, virtual or mixed reality, the use of medical sensors, Motion tracking, Photogrammetry, access to the platforms of main smartphone providers and touch tablet applications, home automation, robotics, mapping software etc.

OUR MISSION

Promote and support technology learning through centralized access to necessary equipment and the presence of experts.

Combining know-how and increasing the quality of results while by reducing production time for related projects with technologies.

Ensure the sustainability of the achievements by setting up a physical and digital archive in the form of a technology library.
